The Plotline

In the year 2090, the Entropy King, a space-faring tyrant, begun his attack on the planet Earth. This attack would later come to be known as the Day of Decay.

In just 1 month he had decimated life on every continent. The world had never faced an adversary of this calibre and most armies were wiped out as they tried to retaliate.

In a last ditch attempt, governments of the world came together to build a device that would harness artificial gravity to create a zone within which time would move much slower than normal. The device was named the Stasis Bomb and they hoped that the immense gravity that would be generated by the bomb would destroy the Entropy King. However, their main goal was to buy more time.

The final assault took place in the city of REDACTED where the Entropy King had last been spotted. The united efforts of humanity were not in vain and mankind experienced its first victory by having the Stasis Bomb go off at point blank infront of the Entropy King. Despite this great victory, the death toll on humanity's side was immense and the continent of REDACTED was rendered uninhabitable.

The region where the bomb went off is now called the stasis zone; a 500 km wide region within which time is slowed down and gravity is over ten times that of Earth's. Over 100 years have passed in the real world, however, in the stasis zone just 1 year has gone by.

The Entropy King was greatly injured but managed to survive the detonation of the bomb. Despite being locked in stasis, he went into hibernation to heal his wounds. Now the Entropy King is almost back to his full power and is currently moving away from the stasis zone in a bid to escape his prison.

The world now relies on the efforts of the Pr0tag0nists, a group of special humans with extraordinary abilities and main character vibes, to avert this impending doom. Trained by their masters: MastrSensei, Magz and Deez, they are now prepared to fight back against the cosmic powers that once plagued humanity.

The Entropy King approaches once more, but this time 10,000 heroes await him; ready to avenge the fallen and restore the balance that was lost decades ago.
